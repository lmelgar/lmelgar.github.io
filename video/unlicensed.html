Unlicensed Drivers

As part of a Seminar in Visual Storytelling (University of Miami's School of Communication), focused on modes of transportation in Miami, I produced, shot and edited the story of Maria, an activist who fights for a driving license for undocumented people like her.

According to Florida Immigrant Coalition, around one million people in Florida drive unlicensed.

Tools used: 5D Mark III, T4i, Adobe Premiere
