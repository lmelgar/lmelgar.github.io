About Me

I'm a Spanish journalist pursuing a M.A. in (Visual) Journalism at the University of Miami's School of Communication. My interests are data visualization, visual storytelling, videojournalism, interactivity, non fiction, and good journalism.

I lived in Uruguay for almost 6 years, where I had the opportunity to travel around South America and being involved in some multimedia projects (some of them even awarded!).

Experience

Media College, Universidad de Montevideo

Writing lecturer

2009-2014. Uruguay.

As a journalism professor in the Universidad de Montevideo, I redesigned the college's journalism final capstone and turned it into a multimedia project. I planned and organized a series of workshops to give students multimedia tools, brought in new teachers and journalists, and directed and edited the first multimedia project done by students in Uruguay: 4 paredes, about Uruguay's housing problems. The last year, a new project was launched: Made in Oriente, about the Asian Pacific immigration and cultural heritage in Uruguay.
