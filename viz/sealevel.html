Sea level rise in North Miami

This project was part of a Geospatial Data Visualization course. The goal was to choose a zone of Miami and visualize the impact of the sea level rise in it. Some social-economic analisys needed to be included as well.

I chose North Miami as one of the poorest zones in Miami, but at the same time with some of the most expensive housings, those in the shoreline.

Software used: ArcMap (GIS), Illustrator
