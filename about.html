Howdy!

I’m a Spanish journalist pursuing a M.A. in (Visual) Journalism at the University of Miami’s School of Communication. My interests are data visualization, data journalism, visual storytelling, interactivity, videojournalism and non fiction. I like reporting about social issues and unequality. And I love music and books, even if it's not related.

I lived in Uruguay for almost six years, where I had the opportunity to travel around South America and being involved in some multimedia projects (some of them even awarded!) If you want to know what else I’ve done, clic here and see my resume. If you want to say hello, or there's something you want to tell me, please, feel free to contact me.
