La vida en la onda

"La vida en la onda" tells Horacio Nigro's story. Nigro is a radio fanatic who has sailed radio waves looking for the most exotic radio stations worldwide. He owns a restore artwork shop, but his life and memories are strongly related to his hobby.

This project was made for the Video Journalism Workshop taught by professor Bill Gentile (American University) in Montevideo, Uruguay, in December 2013. I produced, filmed and edited.
